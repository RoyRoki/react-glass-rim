## 1. Reddit — r/reactjs ("Show /r/reactjs") and r/webdev

**Title:** I made a tiny zero-dep React lib for the Apple-style liquid-glass rim / edge light

> The blur + translucent fill in glassmorphism is easy; the bright *edge light*
> that follows the rounded corners is the hard part (a plain border is uniform,
> border-image breaks on radius). So I packaged it: a ~1.5 kB hook + component,
> zero deps, auto-adapts to cards/pills/circles, SSR-safe.
>
> Live playground (sliders for color/intensity/blur): https://royroki.github.io/react-glass-rim/
> Code: https://github.com/royroki/react-glass-rim
>
> Feedback welcome — especially edge cases on weird aspect ratios.
